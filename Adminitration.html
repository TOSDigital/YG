Prabhakara-Rao

FOUNDER DIRECTOR & CAO

Prabhakara Rao K R is the Managing Director of Srinidhi Group, based in Mysore. He was born on 10/06/1961 in Kerehalli, Shimoga, and completed his schooling at the Government High School in Shimoga District. Prabhakara holds a B.Com degree from L B College, Sagara, Mysore University, a B.E in Civil Engineering from Indira Gandhi Open University, an MBA in Marketing from Dr C V Raman University, and a Diploma in Interior Designing from Mysore Interiors Design Academy. Prabhakar Rao’s career journey began with working as an agriculture and horticulture labourer, followed by roles as an accountant, marketing executive, poultry and hatchery manager, owner of seven transportation vehicles, and owner of internet shops. He also owned two canteens in reputed colleges and served as a Director and Admin in a prestigious hospital. Currently, Prabhakara holds the position of Managing Director of Srinidhi Group, which has six verticals of uniqueness and expertise. Prabhakar Roa’s present responsibilities include being the CEO of SRINIDHI Constructions, SRINIDHI Enterprise, SRINIDHI Interior Designing, SRINIDHI Icon Square, SRINIDHI Ecosys, and AUM Infrastructures - a sister concern of Srinidhi Group. He is married to Savitha and has a son, Pranav P Rao.

He has been active in academics and has presented papers in several conferences. He has been a resource person in several workshops organised by various institutions. His experience with students has led him to explore the applicability of shastras for different stages of student life and also for the related professions. He keeps his interest in a trans disciplinary approach towards our ancient knowledge resources.

YOGACHARYA ARAVIND PRASAD

FOUNDER DIRECTOR, CEO AND DIRECTOR OF INDIC WELLNESS

He is on a mission with a great vision of reigniting young minds towards Indic Lifestyle to achieve Self-Empowerment through Indic Wisdom, Indic Wellness, Indic Health and Indic Healing Methodologies without compromising with their mundane responsibilities and goals. He is the Founder Director and Lead Mentor of Yogakshema Gurukulam- Wholesome Wellness Academy.

Being born in a spiritually inclined family, Aravind Prasad was always curious about logics and purpose behind spiritual practices. This curiosity directed him to Veda Vijnana Gurukulam, Bangalore, at the age of 18 where he could learn the Indic Wisdom through different scriptures and masters. Simultaneously he post graduated in Yogic Science and was appointed as the head of the department of Indian Culture in a prestigious Educational Institute in Guyana until 2012. His profound experience in Indic wisdom and Yogic Science always encouraged him to establish an institute of his own and from 2012 he could share the knowledge with more than 2000 yogic aspirants from across the Globe. He being the C0-Founder Director of an internationally awarded Yoga Institute, at one stage, decided to dedicate his time for personal sadhana and family. From 2019 to 2022 he was engaging himself in deepening his practice and knowledge of Yoga and Tantra from renowned masters.

During the period of crisis called pandemic, he was bestowed with a wonderful farmland in Mysore by Divine Grace through the co-founder of the Yoga Institute for which he is always grateful and this was a blessing in disguise as his interest towards Spiritual Sadhana, Yoga, Vedanta, Nature and Farming intensified which one fine day was blossoming as an intuitive thought of starting a Gurukulam which will be a spiritual community, yogic retreat and a niche for people who seek a meaningful approach towards spirituality, self-empowerment and lifestyle management. He strongly believes that success, humiliation, commitment and spiritual aspiration play a very important role in human life. His skills of communicating philosophy and scriptural wisdom in a very creative and practical way with wonderful examples will kindle a new interest and curiosity to listen more and more. He very strongly approaches spirituality as a life science and we need to adopt a new way to communicate the same with the modern mind. This ideology lead him to meet people with similar ideologies, successful businessmen and entrepreneurs who are spiritually inclined and can play a major role in realizing his beliefs. The collaboration of ideas with other prestigious members of this platform, paved a divine way in establishment of ‘Yogakshema Gurukulam’ which is more a Wholesome Wellness Academy than an age old hermitage or monastery.

SHILPA PRASAD

DIRECTOR OF MARKETING & PUBLIC RELATIONS

Shilpa Prasad, a B.Com graduate and CA intern, was born in 1985 to an Assistant Commissioner of Central Excise (Retired). She has always been passionate about playing cricket and represented her state in women's tennis ball cricket. In 2010, she got married to Santhosh Kashyap, a Circle Inspector of Police, and they have a daughter together. With a deep love for nature and an environmentally friendly attitude, Shilpa Prasad founded Parisara Products, which produces Areca leaf products that are both biodegradable and eco-friendly. What makes this unit special is that it is an "All Women Enterprise" where all the employees, from the machine technician to the auditor, are women. This unique initiative has been recognized by Aakashavani, and she was interviewed on the Mahila Ranga program. Prajavani, a newspaper, also recognized the "All Women Enterprise" and published a half-page article on it. Shilpa Prasad's passion for creativity led her and her friends to start a construction company called "Aum Infrastructures," where she serves as the Managing Partner for Finance and Marketing. She is also actively involved in various social activities and has volunteered for the Isha Foundation's initiatives such as Rally for Rivers, Cauvery Calling, and other activities of the Ashram and Foundation. Shilpa Prasad's dedication and hard work towards creating an eco-friendly environment and promoting women's empowerment have earned her immense respect and admiration.

SHWETHA H M

DIRECTOR OF FINANCE & OPERATIONS

Shwetha Chinmayi has always been an inspiration to many, right from her childhood. Inspired by her father, she always used to love swimming in the river Hemavathi. This in turn made her a national swimmer and her skills of diving were well known in swimming fraternity. Her interest towards commerce and finance lead her to Mysore where she finished her post-graduation in Commerce during the year 2013. Her professional responsibilities in Bangalore gave her a wonderful chance to travel in and around Karnataka which kindled a great socializing skills. With a profound financial experience of six years in training and business development, she was also inspired towards spirituality, yoga and social service by her mother who is a well-known social worker, Poet and Yoga teacher. This led her to be an administrative manager for a renowned International Yoga Institute in Mysore until 2021 where she played a pivotal role in successfully training and managing more than 1000 international yogic aspirants for the Institute. She is also an internationally certified Yoga Instructor in Ashtanga Yoga.

With her skills of business development, public relations and financial management she established a team of more than 20 Independent Entrepreneurs who are international distributors for well- known Japanese Company. She has been a wonderful Manager and Administrator in establishing a beautiful farmland in Mysore which is soon going to develop as an International Centre for Wholesome Wellness. She at present is the Director and Chief Financial Officer at Yogakshema. She is playing a very major role in Administration and Institutional development.
